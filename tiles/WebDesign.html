What

CloudTodo is a simple todo app synchronising data instantly between devices. All data is securely stored in the cloud and you need to log in to get access.

Why

The initiatives for building the app was curriosity about AngularJS, the Javascript framework combined with a frustration of not finding any app among the (excessive amounts) of todo apps available, corresponding with my needs.

So I decided to make my own.

Features

The app is simple but have a range of features incl:
  • Title
  • Details
  • Mark tasks done/ undone as the day progress and let done taksks stay in the app so you can keep track of what you have done
  • Delete redundant/irrelevant tasks to keep your list tidy
  • Colorcoded background color based on urgency level gives great over view
  • Administrate your urgency levels, create new levels with title and background color and delete them again
  • Edit task title, description and urgency level - as the tasks change over time.
  • Drag and drop support makes it easy to reorder your tasks
  • Animated background with panoramic videos

Please take a look at it: todo.rasmusbendtsen.com
